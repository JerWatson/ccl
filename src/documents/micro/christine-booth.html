Christine Booth, MD

Christine Noga Booth, MD, is an assistant professor of pathology at the Cleveland Clinic Lerner College of Medicine. Dr. Booth is also Associate Program Director of the Residency Program in the Institute of Pathology and Laboratory Medicine at Cleveland Clinic.

She is actively involved in cytopathology, fine needle aspiration and breast surgical pathology services. She is currently a member of the College of American Pathologists Cytopathology Resource Committee and Chair of the E-Learing Committee for the American Society of Cytopathology.
