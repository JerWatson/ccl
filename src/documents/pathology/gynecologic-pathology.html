Gynecologic Pathology provides interpretation and analysis of more than 9,500 specimens annually, representing the full spectrum of gynecological pathology, including surgical oncology. Frozen sections, biopsy and surgical resection specimens are received daily. Special interests among the gynecologic pathologists include cervical intraepithelial lesions, ovarian tumors, prediction of chemosensitivity of ovarian carcinoma, protein expression in vulvar intraepithelial neoplasia and applications of micro-array analysis.

Experience and Expertise

Our gynecologic pathologists are involved in numerous clinical and basic research studies related to the diagnosis and management of gynecologic malignancies.
