The Right Test for the Right Patient at the Right Time

Cleveland Clinic Laboratories is honored to share our proven Test Utilization strategies with you. These strategies will help you optimize lab test ordering, increase patient satisfaction, decrease costs associated with unnecessary testing, and eliminate down-stream costs from false positive tests. Cleveland Clinic Laboratories will provide you with expert support, project management, IT support, and ongoing maintenance for your test utilization project.

Five Interventions

1. Eliminate Duplicate Testing

Stops duplicate same day orders

2. Restrict Molecular Genetic Test Orders

Restricts testing to practicioners who regularly order these tests

3. Engage Lab-Based Genetic Counseling

Requires a consultation with a genetics counselor for inpatient testing

4. Notify Caregivers of Expensive Tests

Allows providers to consider the absolute necessity of a test

5. Create Smart Alerts

Provides education that allows a provider to consider additional information about a test
