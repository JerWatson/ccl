Center for Pathology Education

At Cleveland Clinic Laboratories, our professional staff members are committed to advancing the education of medical students, residents, fellows, and allied health students. Training and educational programs are central to our mission, and we are proud to have a tradition of excellence in postgraduate pathology training.

Thanks to the scope and volume of case work and activities at Cleveland Clinic Laboratories, students are exposed to a number of unique educational opportunities and experiences.
